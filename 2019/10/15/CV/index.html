I am currently a master’s student in statistics department at University of California, Davis. I got my degree of Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from East China Normal University. Calcium Imaging Data

Calcium imaging is a popular tool to monitor in vivo large neural populations over a long period with single neuron and single spike resolution. Before any downstream analysis, the recorded film needs to be pre-processed to extract the location and activity of the neurons and processes in the observed field of view. Generally, the pipelines are motion correction, source extraction, and spike inference.

The video is generated by converting the 3024 of 512 x 512 images from calcium imaging dataset provided by Simon Peron, Nicholas Sofroniew, & Karel Svoboda/Janelia Research Campus. The calcium imaging data is motion corrected, recording both somatic and dendritic compartments of 330 neurons.

Performance of SCALPEL

Apply SCALPEL to recover the neuronal signals from the field of view (except “minClusterSize”, default values were used for all of the tuning parameters), as the right figure shows. Compared to the ground truth (left figure, based on a separate anatomical nuclear marker and/or hand annotations), there are 403 elements retained after refining the dictionary via clustering and none of them is zeroed out by grouped lasso regularizer. It is obvious that many small elements consisting of noise pixels are mis-construed even small clusters have already been filtered out.

In particular, when focusing on the left bottom of FOV, SCALPEL is less effective. At the left corner, the data structure tends to be more complicated (left: raw fame; median: soma locations; right: estimates), including dendritic compartments and stronger neuropil contamination.

By filtering out some small clusters (cluster size < 5), 135 elements are removed. Obviously, some true soma are missed as well. SCALPEL may be less effective to deal with neuropil corruption and not suitable to extract dendrites. The default values of some tuning parameters seem not reliable. Simulation experiments need to be conducted for verification.
