About me

Posted on 2019-10-15

I am currently a Master’s student in Statistics Department at University of California, Davis. I got my bachelor’s degree in mathematics and applied mathematics from East China Normal University. I currently work on neuroimaging data analysis, aiming to extract neurons from in-vivo calcium imaging data by exploring efficient statistical models and algorithms. I am broadly interested in emerging statistical problems in learning large complex biological systems from massive data.
