Patient Self-evaluation System for Medicine Recommendation

For promotion purpose, patient self-evaluation system served for new medicine product is necessary to let target customers know quickly if the medicine would be recommended based on their conditions. A detailed chart review of thousands of potential patients about their relevant treatment histories and present circumstances was collected pushed by this motivation. With treatments and diagnoses records of over 1750 patients, a prediction model is desired to build for whether the new medicine would be recommended given disease history of each patient.

Analysis of House Rentals Posts from Craigslist Website

Craigslist is a website that allows people to post classified advertisements for free. These posts span a variety of subjects, including job postings, housing rentals, and item sales. Craigslist started in the San Francisco Bay Area and now serves 570 cities worldwide. Posts are grouped by city to make relevant ads easy to find.

This project analyzed Craigslist posts about house rentals in Los Angeles, Sacramento, San Diego and San Francisco Bay Area, and each post is a separate text file. The pipeline is (1) data preprocessing including data cleaning and feature extraction; (2) analyze pricing based on features; (3) further inference by considering geographical characteristics and demographic maps.

Numerical Algorithms of Solving Linear Least Squares Problem

This project compares directly computing based algorithms and iterative algorithms of matrix computation in terms of accuracy, time and space complexity by both theoretical analysis and simulation experiments. It mainly contains three sections: (1) Research in algorithms of tensor decomposition, matrix factorization involving SVD and Cholesky decomposition, and theoretically analyze their convergence rate; (2) Implement iterative methods of solving normal equations, such as SOR, Krylov subspace methods and combine them with preconditioning methods when condition number is large; (3) Compare algorithms in terms of numerical stability and convergence by simulated matrices with varied condition numbers.

Backpack Inventory Management

This project is to solve a realistic problem: apply Markov Chains to build a stochastic model and compute the stationary distribution of replenishment boundary value to optimize long-term profits based on daily sales.
